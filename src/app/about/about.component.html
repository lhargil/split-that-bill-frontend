About

Split that bill is an app borne out of a desire to simply the process of splitting bill items amongst friends.

The app provides facilities for conveniently managing bill details, bill items, extra charges as well as the all-important function of splitting the said items to participating friends. You can read more about the features here.

From a technical perspective, this also serves as a personal showcase of the author's capabilities with new and exciting technologies like Angular, .Net Core and EF Core, among others. This forms part of a bigger collection of highly-usable and uber-creative apps under TensileForm, a software development initiative started by the same author.
