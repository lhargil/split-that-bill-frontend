Split that bill

Yet another bill splitter, but one made with love.

Split that bill is an app borne out of a desire to simply the process of subdividing bill items amongst friends.

The app provides facilities for conveniently managing bill details, bill items, extra charges as well as the all-important function of assigning the previously mentioned bill items to participating friends. You can read more about the features here.

From a technical perspective, this is also a personal showcase of the author's capabilities with new and exciting technologies like Angular, .Net Core and EF Core. This forms part of a bigger collection of highly-usable and uber-creative apps under TensileForm, a software development initiative developed by the same author.
